The customer inserts ATM card into the ATM machine.
The customer enters PIN.
The system validates the ATM card.
The system validates the PIN.
The customer selects the ‘Cash Withdrawal’ option from the Options Menu. 
The system prompts the customer to enter the amount of cash that he or she wants to withdraw.
The customer enters a cash amount.
The customer selects the ‘Submit’ option on the Cash Withdrawal Screen.
The system validates the amount entered.
The system checks account balance.
The system asks a receipt for the transaction.
The customer selects ‘Yes’ on the Receipts Screen.
The system ejects the ATM card.
The system provides the cash. 
The system prints the receipt.
The system updates the account balance of the customer in the system.
